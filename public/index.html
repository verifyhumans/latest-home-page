GOTCHA (GRAPHICAL•ONLINE•TURING•TEST•FOR•CONFIRMING•HUMAN•ACTIVITY)

Our first product is a new identifcation layer that replaces CAPTCHA called GOTCHA. CAPTCHA dates back to a different period in the internet. The trends have changed, the times have changed and CAPTCHA is no longer keeping us safe its just delaying envitable attacks. Within two years every LLM on the planet will be able to break CAPTCHA in under 30 seconds. This leaves a huge security hole in the internet with over 3.7 million businesses using CAPTCHA. This is just the beginning of our efforts, distribution is king and with our implementation of GOTCHA, we hope to educate the public on while future more complex implentations are needed for the safety of ourselves and others.

This is an effort that will impact the lives of 8 billion people. it starts with you, reading this. It's your families savings, identity, and more at stake. We have battled human threats when it came to this issues for years. Now we are battling a threat that doesn't sleep and will only continue to increase in intelligence and power.
